Connect With Us

1. Contact Methods Overview

1.1 Primary Contact Channels

Leafreign offers multiple convenient ways to get in touch with our team. Our primary contact methods include phone, email, and in-person consultations at our New York office. For immediate assistance during business hours, phone calls are recommended. Email is ideal for detailed inquiries where attaching documents or photos is helpful. For complex project discussions, we recommend scheduling an in-person consultation at our office or your property.

1.2 After Hours Support

While our main office hours are Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm, we offer limited emergency support for urgent gardening issues outside these hours. For after-hours emergencies that cannot wait until the next business day, please call our main number and follow the prompts to reach our on-call service manager. This service is reserved for true emergencies only and may incur additional fees depending on the nature and timing of the request.

1.3 Social Media Engagement

We maintain active social media profiles on platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and Pinterest. While these channels are great for general questions, gardening tips, and seeing our latest projects, they are not recommended for service requests or urgent communications. Response times on social media may be slower than traditional channels. Please avoid sharing personal or sensitive information through public social media messages.

2. Service Inquiries

2.1 Requesting Quotes

To receive an accurate quote for our gardening services, please provide detailed information about your project through our contact form or by phone. Essential details include property size, current condition, desired services, and any special requirements. For landscape design projects, we typically schedule an on-site consultation before providing a quote. Simple maintenance services can often be quoted based on your description. We aim to provide all quotes within 3 business days of receiving necessary information.

2.2 Scheduling Consultations

Professional consultations are available by appointment Monday through Saturday. Our standard consultation lasts 60-90 minutes and includes a property assessment, discussion of your goals, and preliminary recommendations. There is a $75 consultation fee for residential properties, which is credited toward future services if you choose to work with us. Commercial property consultations have different pricing based on property size and complexity. Please bring any relevant property surveys or HOA guidelines to your consultation.

2.3 Existing Customer Support

Current Leafreign clients have access to priority support channels. If you're an existing customer with questions about your service or account, please mention your customer ID when contacting us to expedite service. For routine maintenance schedule changes, email is often the most efficient method. For urgent service issues during an active project, please call our project hotline at (123) 456-7891. Each service team also provides direct contact information for ongoing projects.

3. Office Information

3.1 Main Office Location

Our headquarters is located at 345 E 24th St, New York, NY 10010 in the Kips Bay neighborhood. The office is open for walk-in visitors Monday to Friday from 9am to 5pm, though appointments are recommended to ensure staff availability. Parking is available in nearby garages, and the office is accessible via multiple subway lines (6 train at 23rd St, PATH at 23rd St). We have a small display garden at our office location showcasing some of our signature designs and plant selections.

3.2 Regional Service Areas

While based in Manhattan, Leafreign serves the entire New York metropolitan area including all five boroughs, Westchester County, Nassau County, and parts of northern New Jersey. Service availability varies by location and season, with certain specialized services offered only in specific areas. Contact us to confirm service availability in your neighborhood. We are currently expanding our service area and will update this information as new regions become available.
